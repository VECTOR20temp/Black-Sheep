Black sheep

   Welcome to Black sheep, the sassy little project with a mouth! feeling too peaceful? Need a dash of chaos? Just click a button, and Black sheep will deliver a spicy insult straight to your face. Whether you want to roast yourself or your friends, Black sheep's got your back ...or may be it's stabbing it. Each click will generate a hand-crafted, insult so gloriously snarky, even Shakespeare would blush.

   Don't worry the insults are'nt too mean... probably, but they're enough to make you question your life choices. Just the right amount of mockery to keep you entertained without needing you therapy afterwards. It's the perfect way to add a sprinkle of humor to your day, or, you know ruin someone elses.
